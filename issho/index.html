Our Story
How We Met 21st Aug 2014

It was a rainy day back in freshman year when we crashed into each other trying to avoid the rain. And we hated each other for a few weeks.

- Alicia
The Icebreaker Date 2nd Oct 2014

We became friends a few weeks later, primarily being in the same class. We liked each other. One day we were laughing on something ridiculous ( i don't even remember). Suddenly she says that we should totally go out together. I agreed instantly without even thinking.

- Emily
The Committment 10th Jan 2015

I told Emily that I loved her. It was established already, but no one said it formally before. And sad part is it was over a video call. She was away at her folks, and I was in hostel dorm. I miss those good ol' days.

- Alicia
We're Engaged 10th Jan 2020

I decided to ask her to marry me on the Celebration of our 5th anniversary. It kind of went south, as I was faking to ignore her for a few weeks only to surprise her. In the end, it blew up in my face. She figured out the plan and asked me first. We were both crying for 20 mins straight.

- Emily
Wife & Wife 22 st Jan 2022

So Hey, Join us. We're getting married. Celebrate this day with us and bless us with a long, healthy and lovely journey ahead. Save the date !

- Alicia & Emily
